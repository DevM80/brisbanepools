About Us

We specialise in fibreglass pool cosmetic renovation across Brisbane and nearby areas.

Our work focuses exclusively on aesthetic and maintenance services, delivered through three independent and legally separate stages:

  • Osmosis removal and surface repair – equal to or under AUD 3,300 per contract
  • Fibreglass and resin coating – equal to or under AUD 3,300 per contract
  • Epoxy or topcoat painting and finishing – equal to or under AUD 3,300 per contract

All work is carried out respecting the maximum value of each contract, as well as the pool’s size and year of manufacture. Structural, electrical, or safety barrier works are not performed. Each stage is managed as a separate legal contract, in full compliance with Queensland regulations. We proudly follow these standards to ensure that every project is transparent, compliant, and safe. Our goal is simple: to make your pool look new again, with honesty, craftsmanship, and respect for Queensland’s building laws.

Legal Notice:

Brisbane Fiberglass Pools will operates under the Queensland “handyman exemption” rules. Each contract — including osmosis removal, fibreglass coating, and pool painting — is carried out in full compliance with QBCC regulations.
